This open approach is somewhat like the situation\n in a public library, where staff and patrons might see who\n else was in the library and a bit of what they were working\n on. When Researchers using the Collections log on to the\n same Unix machine using different accounts, some sharing\n of information may take place. While the Archive endeavors\n to enforce its Terms of Use\n and maintain standard computer security, it is important\n for both those who visit the site ("Visitors")\n and Researchers (collectively, "Users") to be\n aware of the open nature of the Archive.\n
